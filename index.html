History of Tharlam Academy

Tharlam Academy was founded to spread language education worldwide. We strive to make learning accessible and engaging.

Message from H.E Dezhung Rinpoche

We provide beginner, intermediate, and advanced courses focusing on grammar, vocabulary, and conversation.

Our Mission Statement

At Tharlam Academy, we provide a holistic education grounded in Buddhist philosophy and modern knowledge. Our mission is to nurture the next generation of bodhisattvas—leaders dedicated to compassion, wisdom, and service to all sentient beings.

Curriculum Overview

Combining traditional Buddhist practices with modern academic subjects, our curriculum nurtures the mind, heart, and spirit.


Pre-Primary Level (Age 4–6)

  • Tibetan Language: Basic Reading and Writing
  • Basic English, Mathematics
  • Daily Prayers, Creative Activities, Physical Activities

Primary Level (Class 1–5)

  • Tibetan & English Language, Math, Science, Social Studies
  • Introduction to Buddhist Studies and Ritual Practices

Secondary Level (Class 6–8)

  • Advanced Tibetan Grammar, English Composition, Math, Science
  • Buddhist Studies: Four Noble Truths, Eightfold Path
  • Ritual Training: Torma, Puja, Sutra Chanting

Special Buddhist Practices

Mandala Offering, Prostrations, Guru Yoga, Buddha Event Celebrations
