Who we are

Why We Exit

At Tharlam Academy, we believe the mind is not a vessel to be filled but a light to be revealed. Every young monk carries within him the potential for deep wisdom, compassion, and understanding. With your support, we provide a holistic education grounded in Buddhist values and modern knowledge. Our mission is to cultivate peacekeepers, leaders, and bodhisattvas who will contribute to the well-being of all sentient beings.

Impact of Your Sponsorship

  • NC 5,000/month – Educational materials and classroom support

How to Give

Three Simple Ways to Support


  • Sponsor a Monk – Monthly or annual commitment to support one student’s education, nourishment, and spiritual growth.
  • Fund a Program – Support vital programs like teacher training, digital classrooms, health care, and nutrition.
  • One-Time Gift – Every offering makes a difference. A donation of any amount helps further our mission.

Additional Options:

  • Sponsor a classroom
  • Contribute to library or ritual supplies
  • Support medical care or festival events
